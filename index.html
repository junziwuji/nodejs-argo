About Blue Ocean

We are dedicated to ocean conservation and sustainable development, protecting the sea of life through collaboration and innovation.

Our Mission

Blue Ocean focuses on reducing marine plastic pollution, restoring coral reef ecosystems, promoting sustainable fisheries, and advancing ocean protection policies.

Founded in 2012, we have grown into an international network with 40,000+ members across more than 90 countries.

Through scientific research, community engagement, and policy advocacy, we address the core challenges facing ocean ecosystems.
